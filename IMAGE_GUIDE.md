# Abe Isoo Portrait Image Guide

## 📸 Where to Find Historical Photographs of Abe Isoo

### **Primary Sources** (Public Domain - Free to Use):

1. **Wikimedia Commons**
   - Search: "Isoo Abe" or "安部磯雄"
   - URL: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Isoo_Abe
   - License: Public Domain (died 1949, >75 years ago)

2. **National Diet Library of Japan**
   - "Portraits of Modern Japanese Historical Figures"  
   - URL: https://www.ndl.go.jp/portrait/e/datas/375.html
   - Contains official portrait of Abe Isoo

3. **Waseda University Digital Archives**
   - Historical photographs from his teaching years
   - Search their digital collections for "阿部磯雄"

### **Image Requirements for Website:**

- **Format**: JPG or WebP (recommended)
- **Size**: Minimum 800x1000px (3:4 aspect ratio preferred)
- **Quality**: High resolution for responsive display
- **File Name**: `abe-isoo-portrait.jpg`
