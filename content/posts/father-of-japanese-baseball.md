## Abe Isoo at Waseda University

Abe Isoo (1865-1949) was a professor at Waseda University from 1899 to the 1940s. He studied at Hartford Seminary in Connecticut from 1890 to 1895.

Baseball was introduced to Japan in 1872 by American teacher Horace Wilson at Kaisei Academy. Abe Isoo was involved in establishing baseball at Waseda University as part of the university's athletic programs.

## Baseball Development in Japan

Japanese universities including Waseda, Keio, Meiji, and Imperial University participated in baseball competitions in the early 1900s.

Waseda University's baseball team participated in tours to the United States where they played against American collegiate teams.

## Legacy

Baseball became a popular sport in Japan. Japanese professional baseball (NPB) was established later in the 20th century.
